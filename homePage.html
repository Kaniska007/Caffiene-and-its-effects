Why was it so refreshing?

What causes that feeling ?

It was caffeine that caused the refreshing feeling. Caffeine is found in coffee beans which was in the coffee powder that you used to brew your coffee. Caffeine is a type of drug which is widely consumed all over the world. When you drink coffee, it goes into your stomach gets digested and is carried by the blood to various parts of the body. Then, when it reaches your brain and the caffeine activates your central nerve system (CNS) which is situated in the brain. The activation of CNS alerts your body and thus, feeling fresh. It is believed that caffeine works by blocking neurotransmitter adenosine's receptors and by increasing the excitability in the brain. denosine is a central nervous system neuro-modulator that has specific receptors. When adenosine binds to its receptors, neural activity ows down and you feel sleepy. The caffeine wakes you up by fooling adenosine receptors. as adenosine slows down the nerve cell activity along neutral pathways, caffeine then comes and binds to the same receptor to speed the activity in those nerve cells. The increased activity in nerve cells alerts you. Caffeine is commonly consumed to manage fatigue, headaches, drowsiness, etc. It can also improve concentration, short-term memory mental functioning, etc.

Is caffeine really good for you ?

No, not really. Caffeine does more harm to our body than we think.

  1. Too much intake of caffeine leads to insomnia as it activates your brain, that is the reason caffeine should not be consumed before you go to bed.
  2. Causes high blood pressure, as the caffeine boosts your nervous system in your body.
  3. To much caffeine can also cause nervousness and anxiety.
  4. Caffeine increases gastrin hormones in your body which is responsible for bowel movements, but too much caffeine can cause loose stool or even diarrhea in some people.
  5. The activation effect of caffeine or too much intake can cause your heart to beat faster. This may change your heart beating rhythm, called atrial fibrillation and this condition is reported in young people taking high dosage of caffeine.
  6. Too much intake of caffeine over a short time of period can cause kidney failure and can cause other serious health conditions.
  7. We saw that caffeine can cure fatigue, but too much is never good. After caffeine's effect becomes low or wears off it can cause tiredness and fatigue. Moderate amounts of caffeine is advised in that case.
  8. Caffeine can also make your bladder activated as it activates your nervous system. So you would be in a need for frequent urgent breaks as your bladder becomes active.
